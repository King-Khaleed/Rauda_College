Welcome to RAUDA COLLEGE OF HEALTH SCIENCE KANO LTD

Rauda College Of Health Science Kano LTD is a private institution established to meet the increasing challenges of our educational system in Nigeria.

The College is located at its Permanent Site situated at Rauda College Of Health Science KANO LTD's headquarters at Behind Kangon Shekarau, Rangza Quarters, KANO.

Our Vision goes beyond producing job seekers. We aim to train job creators, entrepreneurs, and innovators who will revolutionize the healthcare industry. Our programs are designed to equip students with the skills, knowledge, and mindset to start their own businesses, create jobs, and drive economic growth.

Join us in shaping the future of healthcare in Nigeria. Apply now and become a part of our vision to transform lives and communities!
